Guidelines & Registration

We invite papers from academicians, policy makers, research scholars, social workers on the above mentioned themes and sub-themes for the seminar. The abstract should not exceed 250 words and the length of full paper should be between 2000 to 3000 words in English/Hindi. The Abstract/Full paper shall be sent to tojakam1516@gmail.com. The abstract should contain the main research question and mention the title, details and affiliation of the author/s. Full papers of the author/s must be submitted on or before 5th November 2022. The full paper need to be structured in standard format as follows:

English-Font : Times new roman, font size : 12, Paragraph : 1.5-line spacing or Hindi-Kruti Dev 10, Font size : 14, alignment-justified. Affiliation of the author and name should be clearly mentioned with contact details (email and whatsapp number). All papers will be reviewed by a panel of experts and only well researched papers will be considered for publication in the form of a book with ISBN or in a UGC listed journal.
