How to reach Deoghar

By Rail -Deoghar is well connected by rail, road and air. Jasidih junction is the main station at an approximate 7 km distance and it takes 15 to 20 minutes to reach Deoghar. It is well connected with other cities like New Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Ranchi, Patna, Varanasi and Bhubaneswar etc. of the country as well. Baidyanath Dham Junction and Deoghar station are the other two station to reach Deoghar.
By Air - Deoghar Airport, a domestic airport serving Deoghar, is situated approximately 12 kilometres from the main city. Flights operate from Deoghar to Kolkata, Ranchi, Patna and Delhi.
