Deoghar College Deoghar

Deoghar College, Deoghar Established in the year 1951, Deoghar College, Deoghar a Constituent unit of S.K.M.University, started its journey with the founder fathers' pioneering vision of producing socially responsible educated citizens. In keeping with the vision of the founder members- former Chief Minister of Bihar, Pdt. Binodanand Jha, Shri Umapati Banerjee and Shri K. N. Sahay - the College encourages academic excellence, promoting personality development and community service.
The College campus, spreading over 42 sprawling acres of land with the main building situated in the middle, has well-equipped laboratories and library. There are about 4000 aspiring students pursuing UG and PG courses every year. Students can also opt to enrol themselves in B.A. LLB and self-financed courses like B.Lib, BCA and BBA. Apart from classrooms and galleries, the College has indoor stadium, auditorium and shooting range facility designed for firearm practice. It is committed to provide value based technical education by continually improving the services to produce qualified and competent persons and to transform the institution into a “Centre of Global Excellence”.

About Deoghar Deoghar, in the state of Jharkhand, is also known as “Baidyanath Dham”. The origin of Baidyanathdham is lost in antiquity. It has been referred to as Haritakivan or Ketakivan in Sanskrit Texts. Deoghar district is one of the twenty-four districts of Jharkhand state in eastern India, and Deoghar town is the administrative headquarters of this district. This district is known for the Baidyanath Jyotirlinga shrine. Deoghar has a picturesque location. The climate of Deoghar is dry and congenial . It is a famous health resort and a major Hindu pilgrimage center. Baidyanath Temple in Deoghar holds one of the twelve 'Shiva Jyothirlingams' and is also one of the 51 saktipeeths in India.

Akhil Bhartiya Rashtriya Shaikshik Mahasangh (ABRSM)

Akhil Bhartiya Rashtriya Shaikshik Mahasangh (ABRSM) is an organisation imbued with Indianness with an aim to propagate the ideology of Cultural Nationalism in the field of education and society. Along with safeguarding teachers' interests including their salary, allowances, service conditions and other facilities, the Mahasangh keeps in mind its national objectives, plans and executes the programmes of social concern and educational upgradation.

Accordingly, Akhil Bhartiya Rashtriya Shaikshik Mahasangh comprising the range from Pre-Primary to University level teachers throbbing with the sense of Nationality and Indian philosphy was founded in 1988. Now Mahasangh is a country wide organisation. Presently, 35 State level organisations and more than 50 University level organisations spread over 24 States are affiliated to it.

Registration and Central Office : Akhil Bhartiya Rashtriya Shaikshik Mahasangh is a registered organisation vide no. S/ 25125/1993 in 1993. The Headquarters of the Mahasangh is located in New Delhi in its own building. Vision Rashtra Ke Hit Mein Shiksha Shiksha Ke Hit Mein Shikshak Shikshak Ke Hit Mein Samaj.

Indian Council of Social Science Research (ICSSR) was established in the year of 1969 by the Government of India to promote research in social sciences in the country.

ICSSR provide grants for projects, fellowships, international collaboration, capacity building, survey, publications etc. to promote research in social sciences in India.

Documentation center of ICSSR - National Social Science Documentation Centre (NASSDOC) - provides library and information support services to researchers in social sciences.

ICSSR has developed ICSSR Data Service to serve as a national data service for promoting powerful research environment through sharing and reuse of data among social science community in India.
